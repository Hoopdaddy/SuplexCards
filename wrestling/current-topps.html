2025 — Present — Topps — WWE Exclusive License Returns

Current Topps Era

Topps reclaims the WWE license — a new generation of sets for the most stacked WWE roster in years

Topps WWE 2025+ Ongoing

After three years with Panini, Topps reclaimed the exclusive WWE trading card license beginning in 2025. Topps returns with their proven flagship format, Chrome, and premium sets for a WWE roster that is arguably the deepest and most talented in company history. The era opens with CM Punk on Raw, Cody Rhodes as the face of SmackDown, Gunther as world champion, and a next generation of rising stars including Jacob Fatu, Trick Williams, Jade Cargill, and Lyra Valkyria.

This page will grow as new Topps WWE sets are released. The early sets of any new era tend to be the most valuable long-term — the first Topps return cards of key wrestlers will be the definitive modern RCs in their Topps run. Watch this space.

Ones to Watch

The wrestlers whose cards should be on your radar as this era begins.

Jacob Fatu

The most physically impressive debut of 2024. His Topps WWE RC in the new era will be a key card. Athletic freak who the company is clearly investing in for the long term.

Rising Fast2025 RC

Trick Williams

The NXT Champion who crossed over to the main roster with a massive fan reaction. His charisma and in-ring ability make him a future main event player — and a future card market mover.

RisingNXT Crossover

Jade Cargill

The most physically dominant woman in wrestling today. Her AEW RCs are already valuable — her Topps WWE cards will be even more important for her long-term legacy in the hobby.

Key

Lyra Valkyria

The Women's Intercontinental Championship was built for her. Her athleticism and look have all the makings of a long-term star. Get her early cards now before the market catches up.

CM Punk

Back on Raw and fully healthy (fingers crossed). His 2025 Topps flagship will be the first Topps Punk card since 2014. Pent-up demand from a fanbase that never stopped wanting his cards.

Always Hot

Gunther

World Heavyweight Champion and arguably the best in-ring performer on the roster right now. His brief Panini era RCs are already climbing. His Topps return cards will cement his legacy in the hobby.
