First Autographed Cards

The earliest certified on-card autograph for every major wrestler — from 1994 Action Packed to today's Prizm autos

How this list works: Each entry represents the earliest known licensed, certified autograph card of that wrestler — not a cut signature, not a sticker auto where possible. For most wrestlers from the 1990s, that means the 1994–95 Action Packed WWF set, which was the first major product to include on-card autographs. For WCW/ECW talent, it's often their first Fleer or Topps auto. For modern stars, Topps and Panini certified autos define the category. Cards marked ON-CARD have a live signature directly on the card — these command a significant premium over sticker autos.
