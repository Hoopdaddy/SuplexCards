Topps produced dedicated NXT sets starting in 2017, giving NXT talents their own flagship product. These sets are hunting grounds for key rookie cards of future main roster stars. Early NXT cards for wrestlers who later became major stars are routinely undervalued at time of release.

Key NXT sets: 2014 Topps WWE NXT Prospects (early inserts), 2015 Topps WWE NXT inserts (the big ones), 2017 Topps WWE NXT standalone set, 2018 Topps WWE NXT, 2019 Topps WWE NXT (Rhea Ripley auto RC), 2020 Topps WWE NXT, 2021 Topps WWE NXT.
