About Me

The wrestling card guy — and proud of it

Who I Am

I'm a lifelong wrestling fan and card collector who finally combined the two best things in the world into one obsession. I've been watching wrestling since I was a kid, and collecting cards almost as long.

I set up at local card shows to share the cards I love with other people who love them too. It's not just a hobby — it's a community, and I'm happy to be a small part of it.

Whether you've been collecting since 1985 or you just picked up your first pack last week, you'll get the same treatment at my table: fair prices, honest talk, and zero snobbery.

My Collecting Philosophy

Collect What You Love

I've been collecting long enough to see prices go up, prices go down, trends come and go. The one thing that never changes is how it feels to hold a card of a wrestler you've loved your whole life.

My advice: collect the wrestlers you love, not the wrestlers you think will make you money. If you love Bret Hart, collect Bret Hart. If you love Rhea Ripley, collect Rhea Ripley. The joy of the hobby comes from the connection — not the spreadsheet.

That said — buy ECW cards. Seriously. Nobody is paying attention to ECW cards right now and they're some of the rarest wrestling cards ever made. That won't last.

  • Raw cards can be just as beautiful as graded slabs
  • The hunt is half the fun — don't rush to complete sets
  • Talk to other collectors — they know things you don't
  • Every card has a story — learn the history behind what you collect
  • The best investment is always in something you'd be happy to keep forever

What I Love About WWE Today

Current Product

The Storytelling Is Great Right Now

The Roman Reigns Tribal Chief saga was one of the best long-term wrestling stories ever told. Sami Zayn's Honorary Uce arc was comedy AND drama at the same time. WWE is firing on all cylinders.

Women's Division

Women's Wrestling Has Never Been Better

Rhea Ripley. Bianca Belair. IYO SKY. Becky Lynch. The women's matches are consistently some of the best on the card. And their cards? Still undervalued compared to where they're going.

NXT

NXT Is the Best Wrestling on TV

Week in, week out, NXT delivers. And it's loaded with future main roster stars — which means future key rookie cards. Pay attention to NXT. The cards tell the story first.

WrestleMania

WrestleMania Is Still the Pinnacle

Two nights. Tens of thousands of fans. The biggest moments in wrestling. Every WrestleMania card I own means more after watching the show. That's the magic of collecting wrestling cards.

CM Punk Return

CM Punk Coming Back Was Everything

The Survivor Series return. Chicago going insane. His AEW cards. His 2024 Topps WWE cards. The reaction of a city that waited 10 years. That's why wrestling is the greatest entertainment there is.

The Future

The Next Generation Is Here

Gunther. Damian Priest. LA Knight. Bronson Reed. Trick Williams. The future of WWE is stacked. And their rookie cards? Most of them are still sitting in dollar boxes at card shows. Get them now.
