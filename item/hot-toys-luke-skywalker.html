Collector Notes

The MMS297 Luke Skywalker is considered Hot Toys' definitive A New Hope Luke and remains one of the company's most sought-after Star Wars releases. The Mark Hamill head sculpt is widely praised for its accuracy to the young actor's likeness in 1977.

Box condition matters greatly. A complete MIB specimen with excellent box condition commands a significant premium. The box itself, with its window display panel, is part of the collectible. Buyers should inspect photos carefully for box damage, especially corner wear and the clear window panel.

What to check when buying secondhand: All 5 pairs of hands present, lightsaber blade without yellowing, head sculpt with no paint wear on the eyes, and the DL-44 blaster without breakage to the scope attachment.
