To Boldly Go Where No One Has Gone Before

Gene Roddenberry's optimistic vision of humanity's future has captivated audiences for nearly six decades. Set in the 23rd and 24th centuries, Star Trek imagines a United Federation of Planets where humanity has overcome poverty, disease, and war — and now explores the universe aboard starships crewed by humans and alien beings alike.

From the original 1966 series with Captain Kirk and Mr. Spock, through The Next Generation's Captain Picard, Deep Space Nine's Captain Sisko, and Voyager's Captain Janeway, to modern series like Discovery, Picard, and Strange New Worlds — Star Trek is the longest-running and most expansive science fiction franchise ever made.

Star Trek collectibles span over 50 years — from vintage Mego figures and AMT model kits to today's Diamond Select statues, Art Asylum starships, and the massive Eaglemoss starship collection of 200+ officially licensed die-cast vessels.
