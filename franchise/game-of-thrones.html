When You Play the Game of Thrones, You Win or You Die

George R.R. Martin's A Song of Ice and Fire novel series — begun in 1996 — is the foundation of one of the most ambitious television productions in history. Game of Thrones ran for 8 seasons on HBO, won a record 59 Emmy Awards, and at its peak was the most watched television drama ever made.

Set in the fictional continents of Westeros and Essos, the series follows noble houses locked in deadly political conflict for control of the Iron Throne, while an ancient supernatural threat gathers beyond the Wall in the frozen North.

The prequel series House of the Dragon (2022–Present) chronicles the Targaryen civil war known as the Dance of the Dragons, 170 years before the events of GoT. Dark Horse, Funko, and premium makers like Chronicle Collectibles produce extensive lines of figures, statues, and prop replicas.
