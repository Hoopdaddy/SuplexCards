Humanity Has Colonized the Solar System

James S.A. Corey's (pen name for Daniel Abraham and Ty Franck) celebrated hard science fiction novel series is one of the most scientifically rigorous and politically nuanced space operas ever written. Set in a future where humanity has colonized Mars and the asteroid belt, The Expanse depicts a solar system on the brink of war — and an alien threat that changes everything.

The television adaptation began on Syfy (2015–2018) before Amazon rescued it for three more seasons, concluding in 2022. It is widely praised as one of the most faithful and well-produced science fiction TV series ever made — with zero-gravity physics, realistic ship propulsion, and political complexity rivaling the best novels.

The Expanse's collectible market is emerging — primarily consisting of art prints, limited edition books, and prop replicas from the show's production.
