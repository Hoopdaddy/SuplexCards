Violence Is the Last Refuge of the Incompetent

Isaac Asimov's landmark science fiction series — begun in 1951 — imagines the fall of a galactic civilization spanning a million worlds and the secret plan of mathematician Hari Seldon to shorten the coming dark age from 30,000 years to just one thousand, using the science of psychohistory to predict the future of the human species.

The Foundation series is one of the most awarded and influential science fiction works ever published — winner of the Hugo Award for "Best All-Time Series" in 1966. Apple TV+'s lavish adaptation (2021–Present) brings Asimov's sweeping intellectual epic to a new generation with stunning production values.

Foundation collectibles are an emerging category, with premium limited-edition book editions, art prints, and TV merchandise growing alongside the show's expanding fanbase.
