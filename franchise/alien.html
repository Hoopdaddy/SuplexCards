In Space, No One Can Hear You Scream

Ridley Scott's 1979 masterpiece introduced the world to the Xenomorph — one of cinema's most iconic and terrifying creatures, designed by H.R. Giger. The crew of the Nostromo, responding to a distress signal, make contact with a lifeform that will haunt science fiction forever. Alien remains one of the greatest horror films ever made.

James Cameron's Aliens (1986) transformed the franchise into action-horror, earning 7 Academy Award nominations. The series continued through multiple sequels, the prequel films Prometheus and Covenant, and the critically acclaimed Alien: Romulus (2024) which revitalized the franchise.

NECA's incredibly detailed action figure lines — including the beloved Aliens Series with warriors, queens, and marines — are among the most revered collectibles in science fiction. Hot Toys has also produced exceptional 1/6-scale Xenomorphs.
