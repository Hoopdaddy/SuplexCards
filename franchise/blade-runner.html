More Human Than Human

Ridley Scott's visionary 1982 film — based on Philip K. Dick's "Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheep?" — is one of the most influential science fiction films ever made. Set in a rain-soaked, neon-drenched Los Angeles of 2019, a blade runner named Deckard hunts down escaped Nexus-6 replicants.

Denis Villeneuve's Blade Runner 2049 (2017) is widely considered one of the finest sequels ever made — a stunning expansion of the original's themes of identity, memory, and humanity. Cinematographer Roger Deakins won an Academy Award for his work.

Blade Runner's cyberpunk aesthetic has made it a cult collecting category. Premium figure makers threezero and Medicom have produced extraordinary pieces capturing the film's distinctive look. The franchise's visual design makes every collectible feel like a piece of cinematic art.
