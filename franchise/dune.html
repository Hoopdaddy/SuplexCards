The Spice Must Flow

Frank Herbert's monumental 1965 novel is widely considered the greatest science fiction book ever written. Set thousands of years in the future, Dune follows Paul Atreides — heir to a noble family — as he navigates the brutal politics of Arrakis, the desert planet that is the sole source of the most precious substance in the universe: melange, or "spice."

Denis Villeneuve's stunning two-part film adaptation (2021, 2024) brought Dune to a new generation with Academy Award-winning cinematography and a cast of global superstars. The prequel series Dune: Prophecy now expands the universe on Max.

Dune collectibles are a rapidly growing category — with McFarlane Toys producing the official figure line and premium statue makers entering the market following the films' success.
