The Wheel Turns, and Ages Come and Pass

Robert Jordan's massive epic fantasy series — 14 novels spanning over four million words across 23 years of writing — is one of the most beloved fantasy sagas ever created. The Wheel of Time world is a meticulously detailed secondary world where the One Power, the Dragon Reborn, and the eternal struggle between the Light and the Shadow play out across an epic canvas of politics, prophecy, and war.

After Robert Jordan's death in 2007, Brandon Sanderson completed the final three novels using Jordan's extensive notes, concluding the series with A Memory of Light in 2013. Amazon's Prime Video adaptation (2021–Present) brings the world of the Aes Sedai and the Dragon Reborn to millions of new viewers.

Wheel of Time collectibles are anchored in premium book editions from Subterranean Press, official art, and franchise merchandise released alongside the TV series.
