A Galaxy Far, Far Away

The groundbreaking space opera created by George Lucas in 1977 transformed popular culture forever. Following the struggle between the heroic Rebel Alliance and the tyrannical Galactic Empire, Star Wars introduced audiences to lightsabers, the Force, and some of cinema's most iconic characters — Luke Skywalker, Darth Vader, Princess Leia, Han Solo.

Now owned by Disney and Lucasfilm, the franchise has expanded into one of the largest fictional universes in entertainment — spanning films, television, comics, novels, video games, tabletop games, and billions of dollars in collectibles.

From the original trilogy's handmade 1977 Kenner figures to today's Hot Toys 1/6-scale masterpieces, Star Wars collectibles represent one of the most storied and valuable categories in the hobby.
